# /banAccount

> ### Information
> **Launches the ban list wizard. This allows you (an account owner) to delete their account from other servers and add it to the ban list to make it impossible for others to add.**
>{.is-info}

> ### Permissions needed:
> *none*
>{.is-success}

> ### Related commands:
> *none*
