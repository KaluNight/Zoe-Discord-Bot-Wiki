# /banAccount

> ### Information
> **Launches the ban list wizard. This allows you (an account owner) to delete their account from other servers and add it to the ban list to make it impossible for others to add.**
>{.is-info}

> ### Permissions needed:
> *none*
>{.is-success}

> ### Related commands:
> *none*

<br>

## Usage
>In this video, the account is only "banned" to prevent the possibility of adding it to a new server. To see every option in detail, scroll down.

## Full usage
> :one:You start with issuing the command as seen below, just type your corresponding details for region and summonername:
<img src="/ban_account_1.png" width="40%" img> <br>
> :two:After that you will see the verification message with an summonericon which you must set for the account specified before.
<img src="/ban_account_2.png" width="70%" img> <br>
> :three:Open the League of Legends client and log in if necessary. Click on your profile and summonericon.
<img src="/ban_account_3.png" width="15%" img> <br>
> :four:Scroll down to the bottom of the icons list and choose the one from the embeds.
<img src="/ban_account_4.png" width="60%" img> <br>
> :five:After the verification click on `Done`. The next message contains a list with all server in which your account is added. With a name if youre a member, else with the serverID.
<img src="/ban_account_5.png" width="30%" img> <br>
> :six:__You can use the following commands:__ <br>
> ## Commands {.tabset}
> ### `@Zoe kick server-number`
>Deletes your player from the server with the corresponding number.
<img src="/ban_account_6.png" width="25%" img> <br>
> ### `@Zoe kick all`
>Deletes your player from all servers at once.
<img src="/ban_account_7.png" width="20%" img> <br>
> ### `@Zoe ban`
>Restricts everyone from adding your account, but its not deleted from any server.
<img src="/ban_account_8.png" width="20%" img> <br>
> ## How to completely remove your account
> So if you want to completely remove your account from Zoe use `@Zoe ban` and `@Zoe kick all`.
