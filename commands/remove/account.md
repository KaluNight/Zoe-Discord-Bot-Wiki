# /remove account @DiscordUser region summoner-name

>### Information
>**This command removes the named account from the mentioned [player](/en/terms/player). Just simply specify the `region` and the `summoner-name` in the syntax.**
>{.is-info}

>### Permissions needed:
> Manage channels
>{.is-success}
