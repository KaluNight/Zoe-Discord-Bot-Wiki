Yearly subscription - Instructions after purchase

If you're here, you've probably purchased a subscription to Zoe. Thank you very much!

Follow these simple steps to receive your subscription in Discord:

1. Join our official Discord server with the account you want to link to your subscription here: https://discord.gg/BF3gspmZRW

2. Go inside the channel #yearly-sub-claim

3. Open a ticket by pressing the button inside the channel.

4. Send a message containing your Ko-fi email address (if you logged in with Discord, Google or Twitter, this is the address of this account). We will verify that you have indeed purchased a subscription and then we will give you your rank which will give you access to your subscription.

Thanks again for your purchase!
