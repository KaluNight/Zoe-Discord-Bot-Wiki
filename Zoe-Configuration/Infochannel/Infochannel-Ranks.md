#  Infochannel Ranks

>With this setting you can enable/disable ranks being shown in the [infochannel](/en/features/infoChannel).
>{.is-info}

>Be aware that you need to be ranked (have completed 10 placement games) for Zoe to display your rank.
>{.is-warning}

> Click on the corresponding setting :three:in the configuration menu.
<img src="/config_info_menu_choices.png" width="30%" /> <br>
>The next thing you will see is the deactivation message with a description and reactions. 
Click on :white_check_mark:to disable the setting or if you want to cancel the deactivation click :x:.
<img src="/config_info_3_rankininfo_deactivation.png" width="60%" /> <br>
After that your infopanel will no longer contain any ranks.
![config_info_3_rankininfo_final.png](/config_info_3_rankininfo_final.png) <br>
