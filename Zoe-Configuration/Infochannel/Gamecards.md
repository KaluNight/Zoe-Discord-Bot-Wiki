# Gamecards

>With this setting you enable or disable the [gamecards](/en/features/gamecards/) in the infochannel. After the deactivation there will no messages about current games be sent in the infochannel.
>{.is-info}

> Click on the corresponding setting :two:in the configuration menu.
<img src="/config_info_menu_choices.png" width="30%" /> <br>
>The next thing you will see is the deactivation message with a description and reactions. 
Click on :white_check_mark:to disable the gamecards or if you want to cancel the deactivation click :x:.
<img src="/config_info_2_gamecard_deactivation.png" width="60%" /> <br>
